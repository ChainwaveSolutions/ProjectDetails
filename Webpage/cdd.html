Chainwave Solutions CDD Program - Data Collection and Verification

HomeCDD Program

Introduction

At Chainwave Solutions, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of compliance and security in all our business interactions. To achieve this, we have implemented a comprehensive Customer Due Diligence (CDD) program designed to ensure the integrity of our customer base, understand their business activities, and continuously monitor transactions for unusual or suspicious activity.

1. Customer Identification and Verification

Data Collection:

  • Personal Information: We gather essential identification details from each customer, including:
    • Full Name
    • Address
    • Date of Birth
    • Identification Numbers (e.g., passport, driver's license)

Verification:

  • KYC Services Integration: We integrate third-party identity verification services like Jumio and Trulioo, providing:
    • Automated Global Database Checks
    • Document Verification
    • Facial Recognition and Liveness Detection
  • Verification Status Tracking: We track the verification status as either pending, verified, or rejected, ensuring only verified customers proceed in our system.

2. Beneficial Ownership Verification

Data Collection:

We collect beneficial ownership information, including Full Name, Identification Numbers, and Ownership Percentage.

Verification:

The verification process for beneficial owners follows the same rigorous checks used for account holders.

3. Understanding Customer Business and Account Purpose

Data Collection:

  • We collect details about the customer’s business type, purpose of the account, and expected transaction volume.

Risk Profiling:

We create a risk profile for each customer, identifying potential risks associated with their business activities.

4. Ongoing Transaction Monitoring

Data Collection:

  • We maintain detailed records of all customer transactions, including amounts, dates, and geographical location.

Monitoring and Alerts:

  • Automated systems monitor transactions, and alerts are triggered for unusual activity, ensuring timely investigation.

5. Compliance and Audit Trail

Data Collection:

  • We document all actions taken during the CDD process, maintaining a transparent audit trail for compliance reviews and regulatory audits.

Conclusion

Chainwave Solutions’ Customer Due Diligence program ensures that all customer interactions are based on verified, trustworthy relationships. Through advanced KYC services and continuous monitoring, we maintain a secure and compliant environment for all our business activities.

For more information or to get started with our services, please contact our compliance team.
